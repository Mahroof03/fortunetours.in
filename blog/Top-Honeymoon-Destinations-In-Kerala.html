Top Honeymoon Destinations In Kerala

So you have decided to take that leap into a new phase in life? The beginning of a new journey with your partner-for-life has to be celebrated in the most special manner. Among all the romantic destinations that newly marrieds look forward to, Kerala’s honeymoon destinations always stand a notch above the rest. Kerala, God’s Own Country, is honeymooner’s own paradise too. Here are the top honeymoon destinations in Kerala, each fascinating in its own way.

Ever-romantic Munnar



Munnar, the land of sprawling tea estates, is truly a heaven for honeymooners.Green carpeted hills and exotic surroundings are the excellent hideouts to spend quality time with your better half. Take a leisurely stroll through lush green tea gardens or pick some exotic spices from the local shops, Munnar has enough to fill your bag of memories, making it one among the top honeymoon destinations in Kerala. It is endowed with pleasant weather throughout the year. A visit to the breathtaking Anamudi Peak, or splendid Mattuppetty Dam will blow you away.  Munnar is a hill station that will cast a magical spell on every visitor, honeymooner or not.

Wayanad, the Tranquil Heaven

 

 

An escapade into nature’s most wonderfully kept secret, this is what a Wayanadhoneymoon promises you. Wayanad, though among the top honeymoon destinations in Kerala, is a bit off from popular Kerala cities. A long drive through snaky roads with twists and turns, and at the end of it the tranquil beauty called Wayanadawaits you. Among Kerala’s honeymoon destinations, Wayanad is the one that is most blessed with jungle cover. When you plan your honeymoon tour in Kerala, search for honeymoon Kerala packages that offer a visit to Chembra Peak, one of the most exotic locales in Wayanad. A heart shaped lake at the top of the peak is the perfect spot for capturing your special moments for life. 

Houseboat Stay In Alappuzha





Not all of us are lovers of winding roads. If you feel you are better off without a long drive, there is the cozy comfort of a traditional houseboat waiting for you in Alappuzha. Not far from popular cities of Kerala, Alappuzha has ease of accessibility. Houseboats of Alappuzha have a special allure to them. They come in all budgets, and with all comforts. As for the authentic Kerala cuisine they provide, it is simply lip-smacking. Spend a day or two with your loved one, in the total solitude of your houseboat, with scenic views peeping at you from the shores. Alappuzha is among Kerala’s honeymoon destinations that cater to all types of tourists.

Tyrst with jungle in Thekkady




Imagine being in a cottage surrounded by nothing but tall trees, or better still, being on top of a tree house in the middle of the night when all you hear from around is the soothing music of the jungle! All these and much more are possible, if you opt for Thekkady when you plan your honeymoon tour in Kerala. Thekkady ranks highly among the top honeymoon destinations in Kerala courtesy its ample jungle cover. A safari to the depths of Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is the best way to spend your time in Thekkady. If you are lucky enough, you come face to face with elephants, or other jungle inmates. A boat ride in the lake is beautiful way to unwind, and give in to charms of nature. How about your first couple selfie against the backdrop of the serene lake?  


Vagamon Hills for serenity


 

Among the top honeymoon destinations in Kerala, Vagamon is one which has a virgin touch to it. Though a hill station like Munnar, Vagamon is on a less trodden path. So if you are on a lookout for crowd-free but magnificent destinations, Vagamon is your best bet. There are several honeymoon Kerala packages that lead you to the coziest resorts in locales in and around Vagamon. Misty mornings, a slight chill in the air, what more do you need to kindle romance? For the adventure-freaks, there are facilities like paragliding too.
