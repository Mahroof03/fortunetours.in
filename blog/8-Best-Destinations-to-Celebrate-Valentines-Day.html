8 Best Destinations to Celebrate Valentineâ€™s Day

February is the month in which all romantic couples across the world go on a frantic search for the best romantic getaways. From sun-kissed beaches to snow-peaked mountains, there are lovers for every kind of natural attraction. If you are on a lookout for the best destinations to celebrate this year’s Valentine’s Day, this list might help you.
 

1. Venice, Italy

Among all the romantic destinations around the world, Venice is the one that never fails to meet the expectations of tourists. The floating city has a number ofmeandering canals and waterways with pretty buildings lined up on both sides. A gondola ride in the Canal Grande will be the prefect idea for a Valentine’s Day evening. 
Palazzo Ducale in Venice is the finest example of Gothic architecture. In fact, the entire city will leave you gasping with wonder at the perfectly designed Venetian masterpieces. Watch an Opera Show, have a romantic dinner at Cantina Do Spade, one of the oldest and finest eateries in town, or go for an evening stroll along the picture-perfect canals. Venice is sure to rekindle the romance in you.

2. Amsterdam, Netherlands


 
The capital city of Netherlands is yet another amazing romantic destination. The best thing about Amsterdam is that it allows you to travel by whichever mode is suited to you. If you love water, there are boat cruises. Surprise your partner with a candlelight cruise!
Bike tours are available in Amsterdam. Hop on to a bike and view the rich artistic heritage of the city. If your definition of romantic outing is more fairytale-like, you can opt for a horse-cart and roam around to see Vondelpark, Van Gogh Museum and a lot more.

3. Bali, Indonesia


 
Indonesian island, Bali, is a sought-after tourist destination that becomes a hideout for couples during the month of February. Attractions in Bali cover a broad spectrum. There are emerald green paddy fields, volcanic mountains, beaches, and coral reefs.
A trek to watch the sunrise from atop Mount Batur will be the offbeat way to enjoy your romantic day. Or better, enjoy the majestic views of the temple complexes such as Pura Ulun Danu Bratan. You can enjoy snorkeling or scuba diving, or just sit on the shores and steal a few cozy moments with your partner.

4. Agra, India


 
Agra near New Delhi in India is a city that boasts of a rich romantic history. This is where Taj Mahal, the epitome of romantic monuments, is located. When Emperor Shah Jahan built a monument in memory of his deceased wife, Mumtaz Mahal, history was created! Today, this is regarded as the symbol of eternal love. Take a snap of you and your partner with Taj Mahal as the backdrop, and create memories for a lifetime.

5. Santorini, Greece


 
Are you a lover of Greek mythology and architecture? Head to Santorini, the picturesque island in Greece. A submerged volcanic crater visible from clifftops is the most fascinating view of Santorini. Oia, with its white-washed look makes for one of the most romantic places in Santorini. Sunset view from Oia clifftop is unforgettable.
If the blue-domed white structures and cobbled pathways are not enough stunning for you, head to the archaeological sites of Akrotiri to step into the ancient Greek world.

6. Bruges, Belgium

If you and your partner share a craving for sweet things, Bruges in Belgium is a good option for you. In Bruges, you can munch on any number of exclusive Belgian chocolates. This idyllic destination is also known for its pretty canals and old-fashioned buildings.
Bruges has something special for lovers. A lake and a bridge named after love. Lake Minnemeer is also famed as the Lake of Love. The Lover’s Bridge over this lake looks picture-perfect. It is believed that kissing on this bridge will seal your love forever. Feeding the swans in this lake is another romantic thing to do here.

7. Fregate Island, Seychelles


 
If you long for a holiday of seclusion away from the prying eyes of the world, head to Fregate Island in Seychelles for an outstanding vacation. This cute little island is a paradise built on Earth. This is where your idea of a relaxed romantic outing will become true.
There is not much to hop around and see in Fregate Island. Sun, sand, water, and nature is all you see. But every part of the island shines with pristine beauty. You can splurge on snorkeling and deep sea fishing, or go for nature walks with your better half.

8. Paris, Italy

With the stunning Eiffel Tower and the countless other attractions, Paris makes it to one of the most sought-after destinations for celebrating Valentine’s Day. Temple of Love in Paris is for couples who wish to proclaim their affection from a pretty spot. Built on a small island, this picture-perfect destination will woo you with its charm. Story goes that kissing your partner at the center of this temple will strengthen your love.
