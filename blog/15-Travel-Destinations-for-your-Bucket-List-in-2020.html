15 Travel Destinations for your Bucket List in 2020

So the New Year has started to tick off. Is your New Year resolution related to travelling around the world? If so, you could make use of the list given below so that you can tick off the 15 best places from your bucket list.

1. Paris, France


 
The name Paris brings before our eyes a range of fashion accessories. This is not all that Paris has to offer. Eifel Tower is undoubtedly the iconic landmark of Paris. It is an ode to the brilliance of human intelligence. For art enthusiasts, The Louvre is a must-visit. Rare art works by great artists such as Da Vinci are amazing to watch.  There are l other architectural beauties such Notre Dame Cathedral.
 

 2. Hong Kong, China

Hong Kong is another city worth the top spot in your bucket list. From sky-kissing structures to lush green spaces, Hong Kong is perfect for a holiday with family. Kids will love a visit to Hong Kong Disneyland. Victoria Peak in Hong Kong is a majestic vantage point from where you get panoramic views of the city and its harbour. Buddhist monasteries are also famous attractions of Hong Kong.
 

3. Singapore


 
The island city of Singapore is a fascinating tourist destination. This is a city that does not shy away from a lavish display of affluence. Everything here is man-made, but with skill and perfection. Gardens by the Bay is a planned green cover made in a unique futuristic design. A must-visit at any cost. Sentosa Island is a gem of beauty and an indulgence that water lovers cannot resist.
 

4. Dubai, UAE

Dubai is all about shopping. While Dubai Shopping Festival is a temptation for die-hard shoppers, there are several other attractions too in Dubai. Head to Burj Khalifa to get a view from the top of the world. Or to Dubai Creek for a Dhow cruise. If you love to explore old and ancient, take a trip to Old Dubai to see the ancient Arabic architecture.
 

5. Bangkok, Thailand


 
The capital city of Thailand is famed for its tourist industry. A place that nurtured Buddhism surely has many Buddhist relics to display. The Temple of Emerald Buddha is an important landmark in Bangkok. There is also the Grand palace, which is an architectural marvel. Apart from the splendid Buddhist temples, Bangkok has a colorful night life too that attracts tourists. Floating Market where indigenous sellers travel in boats to sell their wares is unique to Bangkok.
 

6. New York, USA

The bustling city of the United States does not need a lengthy description. Statue of Liberty, the lady with a lighted torch in her hand, is not just a symbol of freedom. It is a beautiful monument with priceless perfection. Also the Times Square where time does not stand still for a moment. Although New York is a perennially crowded city, it pays to be a part of it for a few days and to feel those vibes in your nerves. 
 

7. London, England


 
The capital city of the colonial empire has several proud possessions to display before the tourists. Coca-Cola London Eye is the best way to get a view of the city. This cantilevered observation wheel is an unforgettable experience. A cruise along the waters of the famed Thames River could inspire the poet in you. London Bridge that crosses Thames River, and Big Ben, the iconic striking clock in London, are all names that we are familiar with from our school days. 
 

8. Sydney, Australia

The resplendent city in New South Wales, Australia, is anything but dull. Opera House, the iconic building of Sydney, is one that features on most picture postcards. Harbour Bridge in Sydney is one of the world’s largest steel arch bridges. Do you wish to have some adrenaline-pumping activities? Opt for the Harbour Bridge Climb, where you can go up to the topmost point of the bridge!
 

9. Lucerne, Switzerland


 
So far we have added flashy cities to our bucket list. Now let us move to cities that sparkle with nature’s bounties. Lucerne in Switzerland is a city flanked by the Alps Mountains. A laid-back city that carefully preserves its old relics is a feast for the eyes of tourists. Explore Lake Lucerne by boat for splendid views. If you are a lover of the mountains and peaks, head to Mount Rigi by a cogwheel train. 
 

10. Salzburg, Austria

Salzburg in Austria is not a far cry from Lucerne. It is another city which offers breath-taking views of Alps. The city that mesmerized many of us in the movie, Sound of Music, might be a dream-visit for us. For music lovers, this place is extremely special as this is where the musical genius, Mozart, was born. Taking a tour of the numerous castles and cathedrals of the city will inspire you for more such outings. 
 

11. Kashmir, India


 
For the dwellers of East, there is a place that resembles the snow-clad destinations of the West which is closer to them. This is Kashmir, the heavenly abode in India. It is a cozy little place that beckons tourists with nothing but natural splendor. Sonamarg and Gulmarg are pocket-sized heavens. During winter season, the entire Kashmir put on a white blanket of snow! Leh and Ladakh are nearby destinations that offer less traversed paths for tourists. Pristine beauty of Leh and Ladakh will make you dumbfounded. 
 

12. Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is a city that awes us in many ways. From historic monuments to modernist architecture, every corner of the city has something for tourists. Sagrada Familia in Barcelona is a church built in the most unconventional way. Although the construction of the church was never completed, it offers scope for serious architectural studies. Most of the bizarre looking structures in Barcelona are the contributions of the famous architect, Antoni Gaudi. A must-visit place for a surreal experience.
 

13. Maasai Mara, Kenya


 
For the lovers of jungles and wild animals, there is no place like Maasai Mara in Kenya. This is a large game reserve where you can spot any number of wild animals. The area is known for its teeming population of lions, leopards, elephants, zebra, and much more. You can go for a safari drive or a walking safari. The best experience will be taking a hot air balloon ride over the savannah. You can watch all animals without the fear of being caught under their claws.
 

14. Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu in Peru is almost like a dream from the ancient times. This lost city perched atop steep mountains is an awe-inspiring relic. It finds place in the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. This citadel was built by rulers of Incan Empire, but was later abandoned by them. A visit to Machu Picchu will treat to you to an entire world of ancient Incan culture.
 

15. Angkor Wat, Cambodia


 
Angkor Wat is famed as the largest religious monument in the world. A large temple complex with ancient statues and structures at every corner, Angkor Wat will blow you off. It will take an entire day to cover at least a portion of the complex. But it will be a lifetime experience if you plan a visit to Angkor Wat.
 
These are the destinations around the world that you can plan for 2020. A tour around the world can be a life-changing experience where you get exposed to the culture, historical sites, the architecture and lifestyles of people from all over the world.
