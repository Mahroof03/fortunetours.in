Day 1: Cochin - Munnar

Meal Plan: Lunch and dinner

Here we start our two days weekend holiday to Munnar. Pick up location: Eranakulam Town Railway Station / Cochin Interantional Airport. Welcoming by Fortune Tours Representative and board the coach and proceed to Munnar, after a two and half hours’ drive, we will stop at our first picnic spot Cheeyappara Waterfall” then proceed to Adimali for lunch. Post lunch half an hour drive more to reach the destination. On arrival at Munnar visit Mattupetty Dam and Kundala Dam. Later proceed to hotel, check in relax and enjoy a Camp fire with music. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 2: Munnar - Cochin

Meal Plan: Breakfast and Lunch

Breakfast at hotel. Check out and proceed to visit Eravikulam National Park, famous for the natural habitat of Nilgiri Tahr, the endangered mountain goat and this park is built with the aim of conserving them (Subject to operation). Lunch will be provided on the way. Then proceed to Top Station, the highest area in Munnar about 1700 ft above sea level and visit Echo point, a scenic spot on a reservoir featuring an echo phenomenon. It’s the time to pack up and back to Kochi. We will be back to the pick up point later in the evening with beautiful memories of Munnar holidays.
