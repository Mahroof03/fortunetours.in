Kashmir

Undeniably called the Paradise on Earth, Kashmir is a traveller’s delight and is famous for its wealth of breath-taking picturesque beauty. For its snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, bristling streams and freshwater lakes, Kashmir is fondly referred to as the ‘Switzerland of the East’. Kashmir endows varied terrains throwing opportunities for Trekking and Mountain cycling, it also proffers plentiful opportunities for adventure tourism like white water rafting and skiing. 

For an eager vacationers to Kashmir, we have designed unique and exclusive tour packages catering to your special requirements like Honeymoon Holidays, Adventure and Expedition Tours and Eco-tourism to name a few. We also organize tailor-made tour packages that can be fully customized according to your travel spots and plans. Cherry-pick one of the popular packages and we guarantee you an unforgettable holiday experience in your dream destination.

Destinations

GULMARG

It is one of the most beautiful places that can be visited in Kashmir valley. The snow-clad peaks and the lovely landscapes of Gulmarg have provided background for several Bollywood ventures. Today Gulmarg is not merely a hill resort, it also has the highest green golf course in the World, at an altitude of 2,650m and also the Asia's only heli-skiing resort in the winter.

PAHALGAM

Pahalgam has been recognized as one of the most beautiful hill stations in India. The spectacular scenic beauty of the place is the major attraction, the long stretch of tall pine trees, vast meadows, and snow clad mountains is sure going to leave the crazy nature lovers awestruck.

DAL LAKE

One of the most beautiful lakes in India known for its picturesque streams and the maze of water-ways, stocks itself as the second largest Lake of the state. Its three sides are bounded by majestic peaks and a large number of dazzling Gardens and Orchards.

MUGHAL GARDENS

It is one of the best and most beautiful expressions of the Mughal culture. The main endearment of these gardens lie in the scenic beauty around which they are built and the beautiful lakes that besiege them.

HAZRATBAL SHRINE

The Hazratbal Shrine is a sacred Muslim pilgrimage destination, situated on the banks of the famous Dal Lake in Srinagar. Its pristine white marble elegance reflections on the waters of the lake offers a spectacular view.
