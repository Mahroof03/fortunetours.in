Kerala Packages

Blessed with the Mother Nature grace, Kerala or God’s Own Country as it’s popularly known as, is an enchanting paradise full of stunning surprises for travelers and tourists. The exciting climate, soothing stretch of backwaters, lush hill stations, the serene beaches, sprawling plantations, reserve forests and wildlife have made Kerala one of the most sought after travel destinations in the world.  No wonder, this small strip on the south of India was selected as one of the “Ten Paradises of the World and Fifty Places of a Lifetime” by National Geographical Traveler Magazine.

For an eager vacationer to Kerala, we have designed unique and exclusive tour packages catering to your special requirements like Honeymoon Holidays, Adventure and Expedition Tours, Ayurvedic Rejuvenation and Wellness packages, and Eco-tourism to name a few. We also organize tailor-made tour packages that can be fully customized according to your travel spots and plans. Cherry-pick one of the popular packages and we guarantee you an unforgettable holiday experience in your dream destination.

Destinations

MUNNAR

Munnar, is not just Kerala’s sought after hill resort, it is also miraculously blessed with countless mesmerizing attractions and sightseeing destinations. The striking hill station endowed in the sheer glory of the nature is situated at the confluence of three mountain streams about 1600 m above sea level in the Western Ghats mountain ranges.

WAYANAD

With far greater forest cover than any other district in Kerala, Wayanad is an ideal destination for tourists with its picturesque picnic spots, lakes and reservoirs, energetic waterfalls, luxuriant forests, unique historical monuments and rich cultural traditions.

THEKKADY

The very sound of the name spellbinds up visuals of elephants, unending chains of hill and spice scented plantations. Thekkady is home to the famous Periyar National Park, one of India’s finest protected Elephant and Tiger Reserves. The enchanting crisp and cool nature reserve is also famous for its dense evergreen and moist forests.

ALLEPPEY

Called the ‘Venice of the East’, Alappuzha is famous for its renowned boat races, picturesque network of lakes, lagoons and freshwater rivers, and white sand beaches. Well, one sure cannot miss those most celebrated rhythmic backwaters the town proffers with.

KOVALAM

Kovalam, famous for its soft sandy beaches, green canopy of coconut palms, sunbath and luscious seafood is the favourite rendezvous for many tourists. Clear water with shallow bottom up to a hundred meters has made the beautiful atoll a swimmers' haven.
